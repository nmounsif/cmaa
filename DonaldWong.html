VIP Guest

Sifu Donald H. Wong

  • Specialty:Qi Gong, Tai Chi, Yi Chuan
  • Teaching:Qi Gong, Tai Chi, Yi Chuan
  • Date:March 23rd, 2024

Sifu Donald H. Wong, born in 1952, started his martial arts career in 1965 in Choy Lee Fut, Bok Mei, Jook Lum Tong Long, Yi Chuan, Qi Gong, and Doaist Tai Chi.

He trained under many prominent figures in his arts: Sifu Ching Gong Ming (Choy Lee Fut), Sifu Yok Wai Chan (Bok Mei), GM Lam Sang-Mantis (Qi Gong), Sifu Fong Ha (Yi Chuan), Sifu Li (Doaist Tai Chi)

Traveling all around the globe, Sifu Wong participated in seminars in London, Italy, Canada, Bermuda, Bahamas, and all over Asia, sharing his arts and passion with many.

Sifu Wong enjoys training and learning different martial arts. His other hobbies include Lion Dance and he particularly enjoys spending time with his family.

Throughout the years, he collaborated on different projects with Brown University, U.Mass Lowell, MIT (with students), Harvard, and other martial arts schools.
