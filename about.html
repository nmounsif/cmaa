8th Annual Cervizzi's Martial Arts Extravaganza Weekend

Dear Parents, Friends, and Families,

We are thrilled to announce to you that The Cervizzi’s Martial Arts Extravaganza Weekend will take place at the DoubleTree by Hilton, Boston North Shore in Danvers, Massachusetts on the weekend of March 22 – 24th, 2024. This will certainly be a weekend to remember and one that the Boston area has not seen in decades. We will have events and activities throughout the weekend for everyone to enjoy. This will be a family weekend adventure.

MEET AND GREET

On Friday, March 22nd, our VIP Guests will be arriving from around the world and will be at our “Meet and Greet” in the Living Room starting at 6 pm. This will be a great time to meet new friends and get re-acquainted with others.

SEMINARS

On Saturday, March 23rd, beginning at 8:00 a.m. to approximately 3 p.m., we will be hosting our Day of Epic Seminars (12 years old and over) with some of the most world-renowned martial arts legends. Some of our confirmed epic instructors include: GGM Rob Castro, GGM Sixto Ramos Jr., GM Patrice Lim, SGM Eugene Sedeno, SGM Ruth Luka Prince-Ramos, GM Kailani Koa, GM Dan Barretto, Sifu Al Dacascos, Sifu Restita DeJesus, Sifu Kathy Long, GM Robert Crosson, Sifu Donald Wong, Maha Guru Horacio Rodriguez, and Professor David Bybee. You absolutely do not want to miss this epic day of seminars with these world-class instructors.
Learn more about the amazing lineup of instructors here.

BANQUET

We will have our “VIP/Grandmasters Gathering” on Saturday at approximately 5 pm, followed by our Phenomenal Recognition Dinner and Banquet in the Grand Ballroom. Cocktail hour begins at 6:30 pm followed by a sit-down dinner at 7:30 pm. In addition to dinner for adults and children, we will have award presentations, dancing, an awesome photo booth, and our CMAA-famous Candy Table. Our new black belts will also be recognized. Our theme and motto this year is “World Peace and Friendship through Martial Arts.”

TOURNAMENT

On Sunday, March 24th, 2024, we will host the CMAA Extravaganza Invitational Tournament where all martial arts styles and systems will gather to compete in a safe, family-style tournament. Remember that this is a pre-registered event only and will start with early bird registration so you can guarantee your spot and save money on fees. The tournament will take place in the Grand Ballroom. Doors open at 7:30 am and the tournament starts promptly at 8:30 am.
