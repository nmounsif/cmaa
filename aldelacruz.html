VIP Guest

Sifu Al Dela Cruz

  • Specialty:Kajukenbo
  • Teaching:Kajukenbo
  • Date:March 23rd, 2024

At approximately 10 year old, Sifu Alfred began training in Judo. Five years later, he took up boxing and competed as an amateur boxer with the Aiea Eagles in Honolulu, Hawaii. He had a record of 12 wins and 2 losses, and fought in two different weight classes at a 125lbs and 132lbs.

In 1957, he then went on to study Kenpo Karate at the Palama Settlement under the legendary instructor Sid Asuncion who was a student under the late Joseph D. Emperado (the brother of the late Adriano D. Emperado) and received his black belt in 1963.

He teaches at his school: Kajukenbo Chuan-fa Kung-fu School. (Ka: Karate, Ju: Jujitsu, Ken: Kenpo or Kempo, Bo: Western boxing or Chinese boxing)

He was a Honolulu police officer from 1971 to 1999 and then joined the Honolulu Police Reserve Officer Program after that until this day.
