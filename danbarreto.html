VIP Guest

GM Dan Barreto

  • Specialty:Kajukenbo KenKaBo Martial Arts System
  • Teaching:TBD
  • Date:March 23th, 2024

Early Life and Passion for Martial Arts: Grandmaster Daniel Barretto was born in January 1963 and has dedicated his life to the martial arts. Even in his formative years, his fascination with martial arts was evident, as he gravitated towards physical activities that required focus, discipline, and perseverance. From Boxing to American wrestling to his proclivity towards street fighting, early passion would ultimately shape his remarkable journey in the world of martial arts. Learning boxing from Uruguayan Olympian and Uncle Jorge Rivas and wrestling from the legendary Mike Shepard Sr. he utilized both disciples well in the world of a violent Newark NJ childhood. Grandmaster Barretto has also trained with Dai Sifu Mike young, Tony Atlas, Mike Tyson, Pak Vic DeThours, Maha Guru Horacio Rodriguez, Libert O’Sullivan Sensei, GGM Al Dacascos, GGM Al DelaCruz, Hanshi Bruce Juchnik, Yousef Badoo and holds high ranks in several martial arts and urban combat military systems within the US Government.
Introduction to Kajukenbo KenKaBo:Grandmaster Barretto's life took a transformative turn when he was introduced to the Kajukenbo KenKaBo system. Having met in the very early 2000’s a deep and loving relationship with GGM Sixto and SGM Luka was triggered by the indomitable Dai Sifu Mike Young (Hung Gar Kung Fu) who insisted he train with the Ramoses. Under the watchful guidance of Great Grandmaster Sixto Ramos Jr. and Senior Grandmaster Ruth Luka Prince-Ramos Jr. and Dai Sifu Mike Young, Barretto began his dedicated training journey. Their keen eye recognized his potential, and he was chosen to carry forward the legacy of the Kajukenbo KenKaBo system.
Mentorship and Mastery:Guided by the wisdom and expertise of Great Grandmaster Sixto Ramos Jr. and SGM Ruth Luka Prince-Ramos Jr., Grandmaster Barretto embarked on a journey of profound mastery. He immersed himself in the intricate techniques, principles, and philosophies of Kajukenbo KenKaBo. Beyond KenKaBo he has founded the Urban Combat Hunter Tactical System which is a 360 approach to survival in urban settings. Through unwavering dedication and countless hours of practice, he emerged as a true exemplar of the art. They graced and honored him as a Grandmaster in October of 2020.
Heir to the Legacy:In the year 2020, Great Grandmaster Sixto Ramos Jr. and SGM Ruth Luka Prince-Ramos Jr. personally designated Grandmaster Daniel Barretto as the heir and future leader of the Kajukenbo KenKaBo Martial Arts System under their continued mentorship and leadership. Their decision was a testament to Barretto's loyalty, skills, unyielding commitment, and embodiment of the system's core values.
Continuing the Tradition:Grandmaster Barretto assumed the role of safeguarding and advancing the Kajukenbo KenKaBo legacy set by GGM Sid Ascuncion, GGM Sixto Ramos Jr. and SGM Luka Prince-Ramos Jr. Building on the foundation laid by his esteemed mentors, he has introduced innovative approaches, refined techniques, and expanded the global reach of the system, all while staying true to its rich heritage.
Global Impact and Legacy: Grandmaster Daniel Barretto's influence extends beyond the dojo. His commitment to spreading the teachings of Kajukenbo KenKaBo has led to the growth of dedicated communities of practitioners around the world. Through seminars, workshops, and his own exemplary practice, he continues to inspire individuals to embrace the principles of self-discovery, discipline, and personal growth.
Philosophy and Vision: At the heart of Grandmaster Barretto's martial arts philosophy lies a deep belief in the transformative power of Kajukenbo KenKaBo. He envisions a world where individuals from all walks of life can harness the art's teachings to not only enhance their physical abilities but also cultivate resilience, respect, and a sense of unity.
Personal Legacy: Beyond his role as a martial arts leader, Grandmaster Daniel Barretto is known for helping others and putting people and his students first, underscoring his holistic approach to life. His dedication to his art, his community, and his own continuous growth sets an inspiring example for present and future generations.
Conclusion: Grandmaster Daniel Barretto's journey is a testament to the enduring legacy of Kajukenbo KenKaBo. His leadership, mastery, and unswerving dedication have cemented his position as the torchbearer of this remarkable martial arts system. He is immensely proud and honored of his lineage from GGM Sid Ascuncion to GGM Sixto Ramos Jr. to SGM Ruth Luka Prince Ramos Jr to Himself.
By nurturing both the physical and spiritual aspects of Kajukenbo KenKaBo, Grandmaster Barretto ensures that its influence will continue to enrich lives and communities worldwide for generations to come.
