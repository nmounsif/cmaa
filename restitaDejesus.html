VIP Guest

Sifu Restita Dejesus

  • Specialty:Kajukenbo, Tai Chi, Wushu, and Filipino martial arts
  • Teaching:Stick and Knife Defense
  • Date:March 25th, 2023

Sifu DeJesus has been training in martial arts since 1978 and teaching since 1982. She owns and operates Seattle Wushu Center in Seattle Washington, specializing in Kajukenbo, Tai Chi, Wushu, and Filipino martial arts.

She holds black belt certifications in Butokukan Karate, Wun Hop Kuen Do-Chuan Fa Kajukenbo (Faircloth Method), Kudo, FMAA Eskrima/Kali/Silat (Petrilli Method), Cacoy Canete's Doce Pares Eskrima, Pangamot, Eskrido, Chinese contemporary Wushu, Chen Taijiquan and Yang Tajiquan.

Restita is a lifetime member of the Association of Women's Martial Arts Instructors (AWMAI) and an AWMAI Hall of fame inductee. She served on the board of directors for the Pacific Association of Women Martial Artists (PAWMA).

She is an inductee in the Cacoy Doce Pares World Federation Hall of Fame, the Martial Arts History Museum Hall of Honors and USA Martial Arts Hall of Fame, and a member of the National Qigong Association.
