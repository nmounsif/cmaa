VIP Guest

GGM Sixto Ramos Jr.

  • Specialty:Leader of Kajukenbo KenKaBo Martial Arts System
  • Teaching:Coming Soon
  • Date:March 23rd, 2024

GGM Sixto Ramos Jr. holds the esteemed position as the lineage holder of the Kajukenbo KenKaBo legacy, a martial art system forged by the renowned Great Grand Master Sid Ascuncion. The rich tapestry of Kajukenbo encompasses five principal branches, each finding its roots in the rich and expansive heritage of this system.
The Kajukenbo Original Emperado Method, masterminded by Sijo Adriano Emperado, constitutes one of these foundational branches. Alongside it, the Kajukenbo Chuan Fa, shepherded by the venerable GGM Al DeLaCruz, stands as another integral facet. In a parallel vein, the Kajukenbo Wun Hop Kuen Do lineage, guided by the remarkable GGM Al Dacascos, adds to the diverse branches of this martial art. The Kajukenbo Iverson Method, a testament to the innovation of GGM George Iverson, completes this constellation of distinct approaches to Kajukenbo.
While the Kajukenbo lineage flourishes in various directions, it is the KenKaBo branch that serves as the cradle of their origin. Under the visionary leadership of the indomitable Great Grand Master Sid Ascuncion, KenKaBo has become the nucleus from which these martial art expressions emanate. Through this lineage, the essence of Kajukenbo has been meticulously passed down, enriching each branch with its unique potency and wisdom.
He is a founding member of the Four Horseman of Kajukenbo, GGM Al DelaCruz, GGM Al Dacascos, GGM Sixto Ramos Jr. and GGM George Iverson.

Early Life and Introduction to Martial Arts: Great Grand Master Sixto Ramos Jr. was born on June 20, 1942 in the Philippines. From his earliest days, he exhibited a deep fascination with physical disciplines and a remarkable drive for self-improvement. His journey into the world of martial arts began when he was mistakenly attacked and severely injured by a group of assailants, after recovering he vowed to never allow that to happen again, and it didn’t, as he was introduced to the Kajukenbo KenKaBo system by his esteemed mentor, Great Grand Master Sid Ascuncion.
Guidance from a Legend: Under the tutelage of Great Grand Master Sid Ascuncion, Sixto Ramos Jr. embarked on a transformative journey. Great Grand Master Ascuncion, a legendary figure in the martial arts community, recognized Ramos Jr.'s potential and took him under his wing. Ramos Jr. absorbed the teachings of Kajukenbo KenKaBo, a martial arts system renowned for its synthesis of diverse martial arts styles and developed a profound understanding of its techniques and philosophies.
Dedication and Mastery: Through years of unwavering dedication and rigorous training, Sixto Ramos Jr. honed his skills to perfection. He not only mastered the intricate techniques of Kajukenbo KenKaBo but also delved into its underlying principles of adaptability, self-defense, and personal growth. This commitment to excellence paved the way for his rise as a true martial arts grandmaster.
He earned dozens of local, National and International Titles in his illustrious career fighting the top fighters in the nation in the heyday of martial arts competitions. His last title was won in Las Vegas crowned as Grand Champion in 2006 when he was in his mid 60’s! Since then, GGM Sixto has been inducted into 3 International Halls of Fame alongside his wife SGM Ruth Luka Prince-Ramos Jr.
Carrying the Torch: Sixto Ramos Jr. was personally chosen by GGM Sid to take up the mantle of leadership for the Kajukenbo KenKaBo Martial Arts System. Sadly, on the same day he passed on the KenKaBo system to then GM Sixto, GGM Sid passed away in his home after visiting the Ramos home, sadly GGM Sixto was not there to see him. With profound reverence for his mentor's legacy, Ramos Jr. carried forward the teachings, values, and innovations of the system, ensuring its continued evolution while preserving its traditional essence. Now as he and SGM Ruth Luka Prince-Ramos Jr. move into retirement, they have selected GM Daniel Barretto of Honolulu HI to lead Kajukenbo KenKaBo into the next generation under their watchful eye.
Innovations and Contributions: As the leader of Kajukenbo KenKaBo, Great Grand Master Sixto Ramos Jr. introduced innovations that further enriched the system. His insights into martial arts philosophy, efficient self-defense techniques, and holistic training methods have not only enhanced the skills of practitioners but have also expanded the reach of the system globally. He is particularly gifted in Tournament and street Fighting having earned dozens of Titles.
Global Impact and Legacy: Great Grand Master Sixto Ramos Jr.'s influence extends beyond the dojo. His passion for sharing the benefits of Kajukenbo KenKaBo has led to the establishment of thriving communities of practitioners worldwide. Through workshops, seminars, and mentorship, he has empowered countless individuals to embrace the principles of discipline, respect, and self-discovery inherent in martial arts.
Philosophy and Vision: At the heart of Great Grand Master Sixto Ramos Jr.'s martial arts philosophy lies a deep belief in the transformative power of Kajukenbo KenKaBo. He envisions a world where individuals of all backgrounds can harness the art's teachings to not only enhance their physical prowess but also cultivate resilience, confidence, and compassion. He remembers and honors his teacher GGM Sid Ascuncion every single day.
Personal Legacy: Beyond his role as a martial arts leader, Great Grand Master Sixto Ramos Jr. enjoys working, he gets up every morning at 4 am to ensure that he provides for his family and students showcasing his well-rounded approach to life. His dedication to his art, his community, and his own personal growth sets an inspiring example for generations to come. He is in every way, a beautiful man, tough, smart and giving.
Conclusion: Great Grand Master Sixto Ramos Jr.'s journey is a testament to the enduring legacy of Kajukenbo KenKaBo. His mastery, leadership, and unwavering commitment have solidified his place as a guardian of martial arts traditions while propelling the system to new heights of excellence. His enduring impact ensures that the martial arts world will continue to draw strength, wisdom, and inspiration from his teachings for generations to come.
